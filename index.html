Backstory (In a Nutshell)

YOU and your group of adventurers find themselves in a universe where all the philosophers we've studied in class have split themselves into factions. Players must create their characters and choose as a group the faction they will align with. If there are multiple players who want to do so, they may also split into different groups to align with different factions.

The Factions Are:

  • The Humanists
    • Overview: they place utmost value in human connection, relationships, and environmentalism. Their members are:
      • Nel Noddings
      • bell hooks
      • Rosemary Ruether
      • Karl Marx
      • Tich Nhat Hanh
  • The Determinists
    • Overview: The Determinists are, well, determinists. They hold the idea that free will is an illusion, and that everything happens purely because of something else. Their members include:
      • Emmanuel Kant
      • Alasdair MacIntyre
      • Galen Strawson
      • Kohlberg
  • The Teleologists (name still in the works)
    • Overview: The Teleologists are, as the name suggests, teleologists. The polar opposites of the Determinists, they believe that free will is real. They believe in intentions and end results - everything has a starting point and an intentional end point. Their members include:
      • John Stuart Mill
      • Sarte
      • Aristotle
      • Enrique Dussel

Due to their conflicting idealologies, these factions are at war with one another, each trying to prove the other wrong; that ~thier~ philosophy is right.
